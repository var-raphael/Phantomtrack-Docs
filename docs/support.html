Reporting Issues

When reporting a bug or issue, please include:

What happened?

Describe the issue you encountered as clearly as possible.

Steps to reproduce

Tell us what steps you took before the issue occurred so we can replicate it.

Environment details

Include your browser, operating system, and any relevant screenshots or error messages.

Expected behavior

What did you expect to happen instead?

Before You Reach Out

Check if your question is already answered:

Have you checked the documentation?

Most common questions are covered in our documentation. Start with the Quick Start Guide.

API not working?

Review the Authentication Guide and API Endpoints documentation.

Missing data or incorrect stats?

Ensure your tracking script is properly installed and check your browser's console for errors.

Billing or subscription questions?

Check your account dashboard first. For payment issues, email us directly at billing@phantomtrack.com

We value your feedback! Whether it's a bug report, feature request, or just a suggestion, we're always listening. Your input helps us make PhantomTrack better for everyone.
